Check out the [talk](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ad-laM5g6hE) hosted by Hoshua Hodges’s Audio Programmer online meetup to learn how Phausto provides full access to Faust’s extensive libraries and exporters, enabling the rapid development of audio tools.
The session focuses on the Cmajor exporter, demonstrating how to create a plugin and use it in a DAW via the Cmajor wrapper plugin. 
Designed for educational purposes, Phausto empowers sound artists, producers, and beginners by making advanced synthesizer and effects design fast and approachable.
